Welcome to NIAS

The Natural Ingredient Alert System (NIAS) is your go-to platform for staying informed about natural alternatives to chemical ingredients. Our mission is to help manufacturers transition to more natural, sustainable ingredients while keeping costs in check.

With NIAS, you can:

  • Search our comprehensive database of chemical ingredients and their natural alternatives
  • Compare prices between chemical ingredients and natural alternatives
  • Receive alerts when natural alternatives become more cost-effective
  • Connect with suppliers of natural ingredients

Join NIAS today and take the first step towards a more natural, sustainable future for your products!
