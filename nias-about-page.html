Our Mission

At NIAS, we believe in the power of natural ingredients to transform the food industry. Our mission is to facilitate the transition from chemical ingredients to natural alternatives, making it easier and more cost-effective for manufacturers to create healthier, more sustainable products.

How It Works

Our Database

NIAS maintains a comprehensive database of chemical ingredients and their natural alternatives. This database includes:

  • Detailed information on each ingredient
  • Price comparisons between chemical and natural options
  • Sourcing information for natural alternatives
  • User ratings and reviews

Alert System

Our alert system keeps you informed about price changes and new alternatives:

  • Set preferences for ingredients you want to monitor
  • Receive email or SMS alerts when natural alternatives become more cost-effective
  • Get direct links to suppliers for easy sourcing
